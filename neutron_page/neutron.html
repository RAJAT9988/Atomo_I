Why Neutron is the best of device in the world

Central Home Controller

Neutron offers core smart home automation that's simple, reliable, and affordable.

Multi-Protocol Support

Built-in support for WiFi, BLE, IR, RF, and basic compatibility for mainstream smart devices.

App-Based Control

Intuitive mobile app for controlling lights, fans, switches, and sensors from anywhere.

Seamless Device Pairing

Quick and user-friendly onboarding of smart devices with minimal setup.

Basic Automation Engine

Create schedules, timers, and rule-based automations like motion-triggered lighting.

Local+Cloud Sync

Runs automations locally for speed, syncing to cloud for access and backup.

DIY & Installer Friendly

Ideal for first-time users, renters, or simple retrofits - no deep tech knowledge required.

Modular Expansion Ready

Supports add-ons for extending control to IR remotes, basic energy meters, and more.

Proton/Electron Compatible

Functions independently or as part of a larger Atomo ecosystem - scales as your needs grow.

Made for Indian Homes

Designed with Indian electrical layouts, appliance types, and user behavior in mind.
