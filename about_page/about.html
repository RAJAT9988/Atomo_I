About Atomo

"Powering the Edge of Intelligence"

Atomo Innovation builds advanced edge computing platforms combining AI and multi-protocol IoT to deliver secure, low-power, and scalable automation. Our user-centric solutions empower industries and smart homes by bridging physical infrastructure with distributed intelligence for a smarter, connected future.

Vision

Atomo Innovation aims to build intelligent, secure, and adaptive infrastructure powered by edge intelligence and interoperability for sustainable growth and digital equity.

Mission

Atomo Innovation develops a next-gen edge computing platform combining AI, IoT, and adaptive networking to deliver secure, low-power, scalable, and community-owned automation solutions.

Values

At Atomo Innovation, we create secure, customizable, and energy-efficient technology that prioritizes privacy, interoperability, and sustainability.
